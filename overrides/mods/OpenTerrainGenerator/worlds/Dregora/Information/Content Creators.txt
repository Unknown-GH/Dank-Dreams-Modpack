#-----------------------------------------------#
#            What Dregora aims to be            #
#-----------------------------------------------#

Dregora aims to be a post-apocalyptic overworld replacement that tells a story through lore conveyed in books & by the look of structures. Dregora takes place thousands of year in the future after the dimise of 22 century technology (2100-2200) A event named the Blight has turned the world into a desolate wasteland except for a couple lush but mostly corrupted pockets. This creates a sometimes alien environment in a otherwise recognizable world with custom but vanilla compatible entities which I will explain where to find down below. 

As for the technical side of Dregora, it aims to remain compatible with Spigot so while it works and is suggested to be used in combination to other mods (E.G. Quark) Dregora itself will remain entirely compatible with vanilla and also work as a plugin with otg in a spigot server.

#-----------------------------------------------#
#               Dregora's Future                #
#-----------------------------------------------#

First up the full completion of 1.12.2, adding all the structures back in and post-apocalyptic cities. Making the wastelands of Dregora a lot more interesting. Next up will be 1.16.5 and a huge mod compatibility update making Dregora work with Biomes O plenty, Biomes You Go and most of all other biome mods.

Aside from the main development I will try to make Dynamic Trees compatible in the near future for 1.12.2 and future versions and create a different branch for Dregora that will be entirely compatible with RLCraft. Dregora's been around for 10 years and if it were up to me I'd still be developing Dregora 10 years in the future. It would however be awesome if more developers were to take a interest in helping team OTG develop OTG itself.

#-----------------------------------------------#
#              Configuration                    #
#-----------------------------------------------#

Dregora can be adjusted to fit the users need by following guides found within the .jar file, this has also been put on the curseforge page. Namely it is possible to remove the lore, and the spawn structure with relative ease.

#-----------------------------------------------#
#                 Biomes & Map                  #
#-----------------------------------------------#

Dregora has 130 random mode biomes as per v1.9 and 409 fromimage biomes. You could either use https://map.dregora.com to teleport to specific FromImage locations, the colors of the map are pretty much self explainatory. As for teleporting to random mode biomes I have prepared a list of main, isle and border biomes. The main biomes are what make up the majority of the biome. Isle biomes spawn within these biomes and border biomes generate around the edges. (E.G. Beach is a border biome of Ocean and TropicalIsle is a isle biome in Ocean.)
